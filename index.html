How to Reach

We can't wait to welcome you!
✈️
By Air
Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport, Patna (PAT) is the nearest airport, approx. 50 km from the venue. Direct flights from Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bangalore & Hyderabad.
🚂
By Train
Patna Junction (PNBE) is the major railway hub, approx. 50 km from the venue. Bihar Sharif Junction (BSFI) is the nearest station, approx. 20 km away. Also accessible via Rajgir and Nalanda stations. Well-connected from Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai & Gaya.
🚗
By Road
Chandi is well-connected via NH-30A. Approx. 50 km from Patna (1.5 hrs), 75 km from Gaya (2 hrs), 20 km from Bihar Sharif. Cabs and buses are easily available.
